CFD Model Validation

Ansys Fluent â€¢ Wind Tunnel

Motivation

Numerical CFD models are cheaper than wind tunnels, but "All models are wrong, some are useful." We needed to quantify exactly how wrong the standard turbulence models were.

Technical Approach

We conducted physical wind tunnel testing on an airfoil using strain gauges to measure lift/drag. I then recreated the setup in Ansys Fluent using the K-Omega SST turbulence model, ensuring mesh convergence at the boundary layer.

Outcomes

Drag: CFD matched physical data within 5%.
Downforce: CFD deviated by >10%, revealing that K-Omega SST struggles to predict flow separation at high angles of attack.
