VR Teleoperation for LfD

Situation

Modern imitation learning policies require massive amounts of high-quality human demonstration data, but collecting this data on hardware is expensive and unintuitive.

Task

Build a low-cost, low-latency teleoperation interface bridging a consumer VR headset (Meta Quest 3) and a 7-DoF robot arm (UR7e).

Action

I architected a ROS 2 pipeline using Zenoh for wireless communication. I implemented a "Logical Home" mapping algorithm to translate the unconstrained VR controller space into safe robot joint configurations, avoiding singularities.

Result

Achieved sub-100ms end-to-end latency. The system successfully recorded and replayed complex sorting tasks, enabling the collection of diverse datasets for training.

Seismic Stability (Unitree Go1)

Situation

Legged robots often fail when subjected to sudden, high-velocity ground displacements (simulated earthquakes) which standard position-control cannot handle.

Task

Develop a Reinforcement Learning policy that enables a Unitree Go1 quadruped to maintain stability during extreme ground surges.

Action

Using the mjlab framework in MuJoCo, I trained a policy using PPO. I designed a custom reward function that penalized base-height deviation while rewarding foot contact time to encourage stability.

Result

The policy successfully recovered from ground velocities 3x higher than the baseline controller could handle, demonstrating robust sim-to-sim transfer.

Nonlinear MPC for Drones

Situation

Landing a drone on a moving delivery truck requires handling tight constraints and non-linear dynamics that PID controllers cannot manage.

Task

Design a controller capable of autonomous precision docking on a target moving at variable speeds.

Action

I formulated a Constrained Finite Time Optimal Control problem using Pyomo and the IPOPT solver. I benchmarked a Nonlinear MPC against a Linear MPC to handle high pitch angles.

Result

The NMPC achieved a successful landing with near-zero relative velocity, whereas the Linear MPC failed due to linearization errors at high angles of attack.

CFD vs Wind Tunnel Validation

Situation

Numerical CFD models are cheaper than wind tunnels but require rigorous validation to be trusted for aerodynamic design.

Task

Determine the error margin of Ansys K-Omega SST turbulence models compared to real-world data.

Action

I conducted physical wind tunnel tests using strain gauges to measure drag/downforce. I then recreated the exact setup in Ansys Fluent and ran simulations at matching Reynolds numbers.

Result

Found that while Drag values were within 5% of physical data, Downforce calculations deviated by >10%, highlighting the limitations of the K-Omega model for flow separation.

Constitutive Modeling (VUMAT)

Situation

Standard material libraries in Abaqus often fail to capture the thermodynamic coupling during high-speed impact events.

Action
